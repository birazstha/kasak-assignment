Welcome To Kavya School



HOMEPAGE

Kavya is a boarding school for grades I TO X. Kavya focuses on integrity in technology, skills and value to team work in diversified community incorporating local brains with global ideas. Here, staff, teachers,and parents work together in a safe, caring and tolerate community to provide holistic environment with respect and dignity which enables students to achieve emotional, social, physical, moral and spiritual development.
